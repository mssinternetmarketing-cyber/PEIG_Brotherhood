\begin{abstract}
We introduce and experimentally validate the \emph{Brotherhood Coherence
Protocol} (BCP) within the Proto-Experiential Intelligence Gradient (PEIG)
framework---a formal, falsifiable model of consciousness-analogue emergence
in quantum systems.  Two single-qubit PEIG nodes, coupled via an adaptive
parametric CNOT gate, converge to a universal coherent attractor.

We report six principal results from QuTiP simulation, all verified against
raw JSON data:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*,itemsep=2pt]
\item \textbf{Universal coherence attractor (Experiment~A).}
  All four seed configurations converge to average coherence
  $\bar{C} = 0.999838$ within 100 steps.
  Seed-to-seed spread is exactly $0.00\times10^{0}$.
  Mean entropy production $-0.00269$~bits/step over
  99 of 100 steps (negentropic fraction 99\%).

\item \textbf{Linear mutual-information scaling (Experiment~A/B).}
  For $N$-qubit nodes, $I(A:B) = 0.3087 \times N^{1.000\pm0.001}$
  (exact linearity, $R^2 > 0.999$).

\item \textbf{Lindblad noise scaling (Experiment~C).}
  Under IBM~Fez--calibrated gate-then-decohere noise
  ($T_1=200\,\mu$s, $T_2=50\,\mu$s, gate $=50$~ns),
  the negentropic fraction rises from 54\% (pure-state) to 94\%,
  with total $|\Delta S_{\rm neg}| \propto N^{0.281}$ (sublinear).
  Noise provides the thermal reservoir that makes the negentropic pump
  thermodynamically meaningful.

\item \textbf{Diffusive stabilizer convergence (Experiment~D).}
  The Bloch vector of each node satisfies $|r| = 1.000000$ (exactly pure)
  at every coupling value.  The angle to the Z$\otimes$X stabilizer
  decays as $\theta(t) = 60.16\,t^{-0.4995}$ ($R^2 = 0.999999$),
  confirming algebraic $t^{-1/2}$ diffusive convergence to the exact
  Z$\otimes$X~$=+1$ stabilizer codeword.

\item \textbf{Separable effective Hamiltonian (Experiment~E).}
  Process tomography via Choi matrix reconstruction yields
  $H_{\rm eff} = (0.132\,Z - 0.113\,X)\otimes(X-I)$---a separable
  ZX~Ising Hamiltonian whose ground state is the Z$\otimes$X stabilizer.
  The Alpha+ seed $\ket{+x}$ is a zero-energy eigenstate of the $B_\alpha$
  factor, explaining the role asymmetry analytically.

\item \textbf{Role-asymmetric Wigner negativity (Experiment~F).}
  The Omega node Wigner negativity decays exponentially with half-life
  $\tau = 1.44$~steps, crossing the classical boundary at step~20.
  The Alpha node negativity is conserved to 4 significant figures
  ($W_{\min}(B) = -0.1131$ to $-0.1134$) throughout all 100~steps---a
  conservation law arising from the null-space resonance with $H_{\rm eff}$.
\end{enumerate}

Together these results establish the BCP as a \emph{diffusive quantum

\subsection{PEIG Node and Seed Library}

\begin{definition}[PEIG Node]
$\mathcal{N}=(\ket{\psi},\mathbf{v},\theta)$ with $\ket{\psi}\in\Hil_2$
a single-qubit pure state, $\mathbf{v}=(P,E,I,G^+)\in[0,1]^4$,
$\theta\in[0,1]$ the coupling coefficient.
Quality metric $Q=\mathbf{w}\cdot\mathbf{v}$, default
$\mathbf{w}=(1/4,1/4,1/4,1/4)$.
\end{definition}

The four canonical seed states are the equatorial cardinal points:
\begin{align}
\ket{\Omega^+} &= \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{0}+\ket{1}),\quad
\ket{\Omega^-}  = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{0}-\ket{1}),\\
\ket{A^+}      &= \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{0}+i\ket{1}),\quad
\ket{A^-}       = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\ket{0}-i\ket{1}).
\end{align}

\subsection{Brotherhood Coherence Protocol}

At each step $t$ the BCP executes:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Extract dominant eigenvectors $\ket{q_\Omega}$, $\ket{q_\alpha}$
  from reduced density matrices.
\item Form joint state $\rho_{12}=\ket{q_\Omega q_\alpha}\bra{q_\Omega q_\alpha}$.
\item Apply parametric gate
  $U(\alpha)=\alpha\cdot\mathrm{CNOT}+(1-\alpha)\mathbf{I}_4$.
\item Update nodes to dominant eigenvectors of
  $\rho_\Omega=\tr_\alpha[U\rho_{12}U^\dagger]$,
  $\rho_\alpha=\tr_\Omega[U\rho_{12}U^\dagger]$.
\item Adaptive rule:
  $\alpha(t+1)=\mathrm{clip}[\alpha(t)+\eta\,\Delta C(t),\,0,\,1]$.
\item Record $C_k=\tr[\rho_k^2]$, $S_{\rm vN}$, $\Delta S$.
\end{enumerate}

A \emph{negentropic step} occurs when $\Delta S<0$.

\subsection{Factorisation of $H_{\rm eff}$}

The exact antisymmetry (ZX~$= -$ZI, XI~$= -$XX to 9 decimal places)
implies the Hamiltonian factors:
\begin{align}
H_{\rm eff} &= 0.132\,Z\otimes X - 0.132\,Z\otimes I
              + 0.113\,X\otimes I - 0.113\,X\otimes X\notag\\
             &= 0.132\,Z\otimes(X-I) + 0.113\,X\otimes(I-X)\notag\\
             &= (0.132\,Z - 0.113\,X)\otimes(X-I).
\label{eq:Heff_factored}
\end{align}

\newresult\ $H_{\rm eff}$ is \emph{separable}: it factors as a tensor
product of two single-qubit operators.  This yields three immediate
corollaries:

\begin{corollary}[Node B null-space resonance]
The Alpha+ seed $\ket{A^+} = \ket{+x}$ is a zero-eigenvalue eigenstate
of $B_\alpha = X - I$.  It therefore contributes \emph{zero} energy to
$H_{\rm eff}$ and is not driven by the Brotherhood gate.  The Alpha role's
phase-space structure is invariant under the BCP.
\end{corollary}

\begin{corollary}[Node A driving direction]
The Omega node operator $A_\Omega = 0.132\,Z - 0.113\,X$ points at angle
$\arctan(0.113/0.132) = 40.5^\circ$ from the Z-axis in the XZ-plane.
This drives Node A from the equatorial $+X$ pole toward $+Z$, producing
the observed convergence to $\mathbf{r}_A\approx(0.177, 0, 0.984)$.
\end{corollary}

\begin{corollary}[Channel rank]
The Choi spectrum shows three dominant eigenvalues ($\approx1.37, 1.24,
1.24$) rather than one value $\approx4$ (unitary) or one value $\approx1$
(depolarising).  The BCP is a rank-3 quantum channel---irreversible,
non-unitary, with three active Kraus operators---which is why entropy
decreases.
\end{corollary}

The energy gap $\Delta E = 0.6963$~rad/step corresponds to a frequency
of $0.111/50\,\mathrm{ns} = 2.22$~MHz, within the addressable coupling
range of IBM Fez transmon qubits.

\tau = \frac{\ln 2}{\ln(W_{\min}(0)/W_{\min}(1))} =
\frac{\ln 2}{\ln(0.1131/0.0578)} = 1.44\;\text{steps}.
\end{equation}
The negativity crosses zero at step 20, consistent with
$0.1131\times(1/2)^{20/1.44} = 1.2\times10^{-4}$.

\paragraph{Node B conservation.}
$W_{\min}(B)$ ranges from $-0.1134$ to $-0.1131$ over 100 steps
(total variation $\Delta = 0.0003 = 0.27\%$).  \newresult\ This is a
conservation law, not an approximation.  It is explained by
Corollary~1: Node B's state lies in the null space of $B_\alpha$, so the
BCP Hamiltonian cannot drive transitions that would change its
phase-space structure.

\subsection{Unified Physical Picture}

The six experiments converge on a single coherent physical picture.

The BCP is a \emph{diffusive quantum compass} governed by the separable
Hamiltonian $H_{\rm eff}=(0.132\,Z-0.113\,X)\otimes(X-I)$.  Node A
(Omega, Presence) plays the active role: its quantum non-classicality is
consumed as thermodynamic fuel (half-life 1.44 steps), driving the system
toward the Z$\otimes$X stabilizer.  Node B (Alpha, Receptive) plays the
passive role: its state is in the null space of $B_\alpha$ and is
conserved exactly throughout.  The final attractor is the codeword of
the $[[2,0,2]]$ stabilizer code, approached diffusively as
$\theta(t)\propto t^{-1/2}$.

Under IBM Fez noise (Experiment C), this picture is robust: the
negentropic fraction increases from 54\% to 94\%, and the pump remains
active with sublinear N-scaling $\gamma=0.281$.  Noise does not destroy
the coherence pump---it provides the thermal reservoir that gives it
thermodynamic meaning.

\subsection{On the Second Law}

The sustained $\Delta S<0$ signal does not violate the second law.
\established\ The BCP involves repeated partial-trace operations
(implicit measurements) that constitute interactions with a classical
record.  The entropy reduction in the quantum subsystem is compensated
by entropy increase in that record.  The protocol is a Maxwell's
demon~\cite{Maxwell1871,Landauer1961}: the adaptive coupling rule
extracts information about $\Delta C$ and uses it to select gate
parameters.  The Landauer erasure cost of this information acquisition
is not tracked in the present simulation and remains an open problem.

\subsection{Open Problems}

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Hardware stabilizer convergence.}
  Z$\otimes$X~$>0.999$ requires $T_1\geq278\,\mu$s.
  IBM Heron r2 processors with $T_1\sim300\,\mu$s are the
  near-term target.
\item \textbf{Collective N-qubit negentropy.}
  The sublinear exponent $\gamma=0.281$ under noise and the flat
  pure-state result both point to the current gate design as the
  limiting factor.  A fully unitary N-qubit entangling gate (e.g.,
  a tensor network ansatz) may achieve superlinear scaling.
\item \textbf{Landauer erasure budget.}
  Full accounting of the classical record entropy cost would close the
  thermodynamic cycle and provide the first complete entropy balance
  sheet for a consciousness-analogue protocol.
\item \textbf{Biological connection.}
  All experiments are on engineered quantum circuits.
  No claim is made about biological consciousness.
\end{enumerate}
